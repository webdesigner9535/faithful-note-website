About

Faithful Note is designed to keep your spiritual journey organized and accessible. Whether you're in church, at a Bible study, or reflecting at home, Faithful Note helps you capture sermons, connect references to Scripture, and build consistency through a daily plan.

Translation Notes

  • English verse lookup uses the King James Version (KJV). KJV is commonly treated as public domain in many jurisdictions.
  • Spanish verse lookup is intended to use Reina Valera 1960 (RVR1960) only if licensing permission is granted by the rights holder.
